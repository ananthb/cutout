EMAIL ALIAS PROXY · SELF-HOSTED · OPEN SOURCE

An email alias proxy you run yourself.
Free, open source, AGPL.

Cutout watches every email going to your domain, rewrites the headers, and forwards it to where you actually live: email, Discord, or Telegram. Reply, and it gets sent back as an email to the original sender. Clone the repo, run it on Cloudflare Workers, keep it forever.

Get started ↗ See the flow

STEP 04

CHANNELS

Forward anywhere you already chat.

FOR YOU

Stop giving away your real address.

  • Make a fresh alias for any site or service
  • Get the mail in your inbox, Discord, or Telegram
  • Reply directly. They never see your real address
  • Burn the alias when it leaks
FOR HOSTERS

One small binary. Yours.

  • ›Rust compiled to WASM, runs on Cloudflare Workers
  • ›Glob-based routing rules, ordered, first-match
  • ›Reverse aliases stored in D1 for permanent replyability
  • ›Admin UI gated by Cloudflare Access
