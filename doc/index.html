Cutout

A transparent email alias proxy built entirely on Cloudflare Workers.

What is Cutout?

Cutout is a self-hosted email alias service — like SimpleLogin or addy.io — that runs entirely on Cloudflare's edge infrastructure. No servers, no containers, no monthly VM bills.

How it works

  1. You own one or more domains with Cloudflare Email Routing (inbound) and Email Service (outbound) enabled
  2. Write rules in the /manage UI — glob patterns on the address's local part and domain, with a Forward or Drop action; first-match-wins, with a catch-all Drop pinned to the end
  3. Forwarded destinations must confirm a one-time verification link before mail is sent to them
  4. Mail matching a Forward rule is rewritten through a disposable reply+<uuid>@yourdomain.com reverse-alias and sent on to the verified destination
  5. Your replies to that reverse-alias are routed back to the original sender so your real address never leaves your inbox

Built on

  • Cloudflare Workers — Rust compiled to WebAssembly
  • Email Routing — inbound email handling
  • Email Service — outbound via the send_email binding, delivers to any recipient
  • KV — ordered rule list, reverse-alias mappings, verified destinations, pending tokens
  • Cloudflare Access — protects the admin UI on /manage
  • HTMX — lightweight admin management page
